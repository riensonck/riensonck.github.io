Deducation

Role: Co-Founder

Years: 2016 - 2018

Description: Deducation was a social-oriented student startup, which I co-founded with 3 friends. Together we built a co-creation platform, used by 2000 university students. On this platform, students could practice course materials by completing a range of exercises, and this all free of charge. Across the years, our team expanded to 14 volunteers. We also developed workshops using the World Economic Formum's Future of Skills Framework. Our last workshop allowed young professionals to participate in learning about decision-making techniques using digital skills.

Code City

Role: Programming Coach

Years: 2018

Description: CodeCity is a social initiative from the city of Ghent and Digipolis to help children to get acquainted with coding and computational thinking. As a programming coach, we were sent to different schools to give children a "coding hour". I coached 67 children to playfully learn the basics of computational thinking and coding.

Ghent Model United Nations

Role: Support Staff

Years: 2018 - 2019

Description: GhentMUN is the MUN conference of Ghent founded by two friends. GhentMUN cooperated with Ghent University (UGent) and the United Nations Youth Association of Ghent (UNYA Gent). During the 2018 Anual Conference, I helped with setting up social events, clearning, registration, and overseeing the delegates. I also helped supporting the 2019 Annual Conference.

Ghent University

Role: Student Representative

Years: 2016 - 2017

Description: As a student representative of the faculty of Psychological and Educational Science I focused on trying to build a supportive student community in coorperation with Deducation. One project we organized were Facebook groups where students could ask questions and share notes. Each bachelor year had it's own individual facebookgroup, with an average of 1200 students in each group.
